Many may know what the Bible says, not many know what the Bible actually means!

"What is Man? A Definitive Explanation of the Origins and Creation of Man" is an exploration of the creation and existence of man as a body, soul, and spirit being.

In this book you will discover:

  • You are a mirror reflection of the Creator
  • Why love and hate exists and why man was created
  • The Bible's symbolism that affects our lives on earth
  • Mysteries concerning the Biblical characters, teachings and ultimate goal of why the world exists

The author brings down the Word of God from distant and loftly secrets to understandable units of living a useful life that advances the kingdom of God on earth. This passionate account surpasses race, language, religion, culture and personal posessions; yet shows how all abundance is open to man from God's instruction in the Bible, often hidden in symbols.
